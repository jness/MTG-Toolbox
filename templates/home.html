Welcome to MTGToolbook!


MTGToolbook is a tool for providing Trending Prices, Image pHash, and other userful features for collection Magic: the Gathering cards.

Data collected on MTGToolbook is obtained from magiccards.info, and prices are pulled from TCGPlayer.com.
Links to Wizards of the Coast's Gatherer are also found throughout MTGToolbook.
